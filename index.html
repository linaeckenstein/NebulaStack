TotalAV Login Page – Manage Your Antivirus Account

How to Access the Total AV Login My Account Page

  1. Visit the official site: www.totalav.com
  2. Click the red Login button in the top-right corner.
  3. Enter your registered email address.
  4. Type your secure password and click Login.
  5. Access your dashboard to manage protection, devices, and settings.

Mobile Login: iOS and Android

You can also access your account using a mobile browser or the TotalAV app:

Resetting a Forgotten Password

  1. On the login page, click “Forgotten Password?”
  2. Enter your email and wait for the reset link.
  3. Create a strong new password and login again.

Enable 2-Factor Authentication

TotalAV offers 2FA as an added layer of security. Go to your account settings, enable the feature, and link it to your phone number or email. This protects your account even if someone gets your password.
